About Me

I am Kinlo, commonly referred to as Professor. I am a Computer Science Major and Data Science Minor student at Constructor University in Bremen, Germany. I am a tech enthusiast who loves to learn and teach. I teach math and science-related subjects on my YouTube channel KET Academy. I am also the founder of a social organization "KET Foundation," which is dedicated to leveraging technology to empower youths with digital technology skills for personal and professional development and to ease access to educational resources and opportunities to students.

Other activities I love doing during my free time is playing table tennis, football and chess
